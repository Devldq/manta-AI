# Design System Inspired by Ferrari

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Ferrari's website is a luxury automotive brand with a dramatic light-dark alternation. The design uses Absolute Black hero sections contrasted with pure white editorial panels, with Ferrari Red as the sole accent.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Absolute Black (`#000000`) hero zones — cinematic immersion
- Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) editorial sections
- Ferrari Red (`#DA291C`) — exclusive CTA accent
- 2px border radius — almost rectangular, precision-focused
- Light-dark alternation — creates visual rhythm
- Ferrari Red ONLY for Buy CTAs

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **Ferrari Red** (`#DA291C`): Sole accent, CTAs
- **Dark Red** (`#B01E0A`): Hover/pressed states
- **Absolute Black** (`#000000`): Hero backgrounds
- **Pure White** (`#FFFFFF`): Editorial surfaces

### Secondary
- **Carbon Gray** (`#202020`): Secondary surfaces

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Primary**: `FerrariSans` — proprietary typeface
- **Body**: Default sans-serif

### Hierarchy
| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Display | 64px | 500 | 1.00 |
| Section | 40px | 500 | 1.20 |
| Body | 16px | 400 | 1.50 |

### Principles
- Weight 500 as default — medium is the new regular
- Uppercase labels with 1px letter-spacing
- Tight display line-heights (1.00-1.30)

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
**Primary Red**
- Background: `#DA291C`
- Radius: 2px — almost rectangular
- Hover: `#B01E0A` (Dark Red)

### Cards
- Background alternates between black and white
- 0-2px radius — sharp corners

## 5. Layout Principles

- Light-dark section alternation
- 2px maximum radius
- Grid-based editorial layouts

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- No shadows
- Contrast creates depth
- Surface layering

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use Ferrari Red ONLY for primary CTAs
- Alternate light and dark sections
- Keep corners sharp (2px max)

### Don't
- Use accent colors beyond red
- Add decorative gradients
- Apply pill-shaped buttons
