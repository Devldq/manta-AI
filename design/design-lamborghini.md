# Design System Inspired by Lamborghini

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Lamborghini's website is a cathedral of darkness — a digital stage where jet-black surfaces stretch infinitely. The page is almost entirely black. Into this abyss, white type and Lamborghini Gold (`#FFC000`) are deployed with surgical precision.

**Key Characteristics:**
- True black (`#000000`) dominant surfaces with white and gold
- LamboType custom Neo-Grotesk font with 12° angled terminals
- Lamborghini Gold (`#FFC000`) as sole accent — exclusively for primary CTA buttons
- All-uppercase display typography at extreme scales (120px)
- Zero border-radius on buttons — sharp, angular rectangles
- Full-viewport video heroes with cinematic content

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **Lamborghini Gold** (`#FFC000`): Signature accent for primary CTAs
- **Pure White** (`#FFFFFF`): Primary text on dark surfaces

### Surface
- **Absolute Black** (`#000000`): Dominant surface
- **Charcoal** (`#202020`): Elevated dark surface for cards

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Display & UI**: `LamboType` — custom Neo-Grotesk with 12° angled terminals

### Hierarchy
| Role | Size | Weight | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Hero Display | 120px | 400 | Uppercase |
| Display 2 | 80px | 400 | Uppercase |
| Body | 16px | 400 | Mixed case |

### Principles
- **ALL-CAPS default**: Display headings universally uppercase
- **Zero border-radius**: Sharp, angular forms

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
**Gold Accent CTA**
- Background: `#FFC000`
- Text: `#000000`
- Border-radius: 0px

**Transparent Ghost**
- Background: transparent
- Border: `1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.5)`

## 5. Layout Principles

- Zero border-radius on all containers and buttons

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Abyss: `#000000`
- Surface: `#202020`
- Overlay: `rgba(0,0,0,0.7)`

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use Lamborghini Gold exclusively for primary CTAs
- Set all display headings in uppercase

### Don't
- Introduce additional accent colors beyond gold
- Apply border-radius to buttons or cards
