# Design System Inspired by Intercom

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

Intercom's website is a customer service platform with a warm, conversational design language. The palette uses warm oatmeal tones with a signature Fin Orange accent color.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Warm cream canvas (`#faf9f6`) — inviting, not sterile
- Fin Orange (`#ff5600`) as single accent — CTAs, key moments
- Off Black (`#111111`) — primary text and buttons
- 4px border radius — sharp, geometric forms
- Buttons with scale transforms: hover (1.1×), active (0.85×)
- Warm oatmeal borders (`#dedbd6`)

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **Off Black** (`#111111`): Primary text, buttons
- **Warm Cream** (`#faf9f6`): Page background
- **Fin Orange** (`#ff5600`): Brand accent, CTAs

### Secondary
- **Oatmeal Border** (`#dedbd6`): Container borders
- **Warm Gray** (`#6b6b6b`): Secondary text

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Primary**: `Saans` — geometric sans-serif

### Hierarchy
| Role | Size | Weight | Letter Spacing |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| Display Hero | 80px | 400 | -2.4px |
| Section | 48px | 400 | -1.2px |
| Body | 16px | 400 | normal |

### Principles
- Extreme negative tracking at display sizes
- Tight line-height for headlines (1.00)
- Relaxed body line-height (1.50)

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
**Primary Orange**
- Background: `#ff5600`
- Radius: 4px
- Hover: scale(1.1)
- Active: scale(0.85)

### Cards
- Background: Warm Cream
- Border: 1px solid `#dedbd6`
- Radius: 4px

## 5. Layout Principles

- 4px base radius — sharp, precise
- Generous section padding
- Warm, conversational spacing

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Minimal shadows
- Border-based containment
- Flat, approachable design

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use Fin Orange sparingly for CTAs
- Maintain warm cream backgrounds
- Apply 4px radius consistently

### Don't
- Introduce cool colors
- Use heavy shadows
