# Design System Inspired by BMW

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

BMW's website embodies German engineering aesthetics with dark premium surfaces and a commanding brand presence.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Dark premium surfaces — German engineering
- BMW Blue (`#1c69d4`) — commanding brand accent
- BMWTypeNextLatin — proprietary typeface
- Zero border-radius — precision corners
- Tight line-heights (1.15-1.30)

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary
- **BMW Blue** (`#1c69d4`): Brand accent, CTAs
- **Carbon Black** (`#000000`): Dark surfaces
- **Pure White** (`#ffffff`): Text on dark

### Secondary
- **Silver Gray** (`#666666`): Secondary text

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family
- **Primary**: `BMWTypeNextLatin` — proprietary

### Hierarchy
| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Display | 64px | 400 | 1.15 |
| Section | 40px | 400 | 1.20 |
| Body | 16px | 400 | 1.50 |

### Principles
- Tight display line-heights (1.15-1.30)
- Zero letter-spacing adjustments
- Scale-based hierarchy

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons
**Primary Blue**
- Background: `#1c69d4`
- Radius: 0px — sharp corners
- Hover: opacity 0.8

### Cards
- Dark backgrounds
- Zero radius
- Border-based containment

## 5. Layout Principles

- Zero border-radius throughout
- Engineering-grade precision
- Grid-based layouts

## 6. Depth & Elevation

- Minimal shadows
- Surface layering
- Premium dark atmosphere

## 7. Do's and Don'ts

### Do
- Use BMW Blue for primary CTAs
- Keep corners sharp (0px)
- Maintain tight line-heights

### Don't
- Add border-radius
- Introduce competing accent colors
- Use heavy shadows
